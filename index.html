Point of view

Growth creates complexity. Complexity breaks the way decisions get made. Most organizations don't see it until the damage is done.

Between $10M and $200M in revenue, something predictable happens. The decision structures that drove early growth stop scaling. Margins become unpredictable. Escalations multiply. Leaders run on intuition because the information infrastructure can't keep up.

This is not a technology problem. It is not a talent problem. It is a structural problem — and it requires executive-level intervention to fix.

Northlight diagnoses the structural failure, redesigns the decision infrastructure, and — where AI can operationalize the fix — builds the systems that make it last.

Proprietary methodology

The Northlight
Decision Framework

Every Northlight engagement is anchored in VDDM — Value-Driven Decision Making — a proprietary framework developed through doctoral research in organizational decision systems. It translates abstract executive values into quantified, defensible decision infrastructure.

01
Stakeholder mapping

Distinguish who holds decision authority from who bears the consequences. Surface the hidden value conflicts driving escalation and stall.

02
Value elicitation

Use laddering techniques to move past stated goals. Uncover Core value, Utility (how we measure it), and Salience (how much it matters, to whom).

03
Objective engineering

Convert abstract values into specific, measurable objectives. Make implicit tradeoffs explicit and shared. Leadership stops negotiating and starts deciding.

04
Network quantification

Build a unified Objective Network that identifies the highest-impact pathways to the primary business goal — the map leadership has been missing.

05
Alternative generation

Score operational strategies against the quantified value network. Decisions are now defensible — to the board, to investors, to the team.

Where AI can operationalize the decision framework — automating information flows, surfacing the right data at the right decision moment, or building systems that enforce the new decision logic — Northlight builds it. Using Claude Code, agentic AI systems, and the full modern stack, Northlight builds the infrastructure that makes the change permanent.
